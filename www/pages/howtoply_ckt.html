  • Create your Team

    Step 1: Select a Match

    Select any upcoming match from the list of ongoing and upcoming cricket series and click on the‘Create Team’ button.
    Create an unbeatable team by selecting your players with any of the 5 possible combinations as mentioned below

    Player Type C1 C2 C3 C4 C5
    Raiders 1 2 2 3 3
    All Rounders 2 1 2 1 2
    Defenders 4 4 3 3 2
    Totle 7 7 7 7 7

    Step 2: Create your Team

    Remember, you can select a maximum of 5 players from 1 side in your team. Here’s what the Kabaddi field looks like. Make sure understand every aspect of it before creating your team!

    • Defenders (DEF) : Pick minimum 2 & maximum 4
    • All-rounders (AR) : Pick minimum 1 & maximum 2
    • Raiders (RAID) : Pick minimum 1 & maximum 3

    Keep an eye on:

    • - Number of players added to your team
    • - Available credits
    • - The deadline for team submission

    Step 3: Select your Star11fantasy team’s Captain & Vice Captain

    After creating your Star11fantasy team, choose a Captain & Vice Captain for the team.

    • Captain - Gets 2x points scored by him in the actual game
    • Vice Captain - Gets 1.5x points scored by him in the actual game

    Step 4: Select your Star11fantasy team’s Captain & Vice Captain

    You can create upto 6 teams per match and choose to join a contests with any of the teams created!
    Click on 'CREATE TEAM *' for creating next team

  • Fantasy Cricket Points System

    Here’s how your Star11fantasy team earns Fantasy points:

    Action Points
    Being part of the Starting 7
    Making a substitute appearance
    Each successful raid touch point
    Raid Bonus
    Each successful tackle
    Pushing all out (starting 7)
    Getting all out (starting 7)
    Green Card
    Yellow Card Red Card
    Red Card
    • The player you choose to be your team’s Captain will receive 2 times the points
    • - The Vice-Captain will receive 1.5 times the points for his performance
    • - Points for substitutes will be considered only for the first time he enters the field of play
    • - Points for super tackle will be added to the points of a successful tackle (5 pts for successful +4 pts for super tackle)
    • - Points for raid touch will be given to the raider for every opponent he gets out or puts out
    • Points for pushing all out will only be considered for players who are part of the starting
    • - Points for getting all out will only be considered for players who are part of the starting 7
    • - If a player receives any card, he will continue to get negative (for Getting All out) and positive (for Pushing All out) points
    • - Points deducted for red card include deduction for yellow card and green card. Similarly, point deducted for yellow card include deductions for green card.
    • - Any event during extra time will be considered for awarding points.
    • - Any event during Golden Raids will not be considered for awarding points
    • - Data is provided by reliable sources and once the point have been marked as completed i.e. winners have been declared, no further adjustments will be made. Points awarded live in-game are subject to change as long as the status is under review
    • - In case of any transfer of a real-life player in the intervening period between scheduled updates, such transfer shall not be reflected in the roster of players until the next scheduled update. Pease note that while the transferred player will be available for selection in the transferor team’s roster of players in a Contest during the intervening period, no points will be attributed to such transferred player in the course of such Contest.

    How do I play Fantasy Kabaddi?
    Follow these simple steps to play the creating your team on Star11fantasy:

    • Register/Log in on fantasykabaddi.Star11fantasy.com
    • Click on the ‘Create Team’ button for the match you want to participate in
    • Select your team of 7 players (including a Captain and Vice-captain) within the budget of 100 credits from both teams
    • Join cash or practice contests of your choice. Win big in cash contests and hone your skills in practice contests
    • Once the match starts, your team starts earning points on the basis of actual performance of the players selected by you. Final points, rankings and winners are declared after the end of the match

    How do I select my team?
    After logging into your Star11fantasy account, click on ‘Create Team’ for the particular match. Select your team of 7 players (including a Captain and Vice-captain) from both teams within a budget of 100 credits. For more guidance, please click on the ‘Creating Your Team’ section.

    Is it mandatory to change my team for future matches?
    Yes, you are required to create a new team for every match on Star11fantasy

    What if one of my chosen players does not play in the match?
    The player who does not feature in the starting 7 in the match does not get any points. This is where research and skill comes into play. Pick the right players, score more points!

    How many players are needed to create a team?
    You need to select 7 players to create a team, which will include 2-4 Defenders, 1-3 Raiders and 1-2 All-rounders. Please click on the ‘Creating Your Team’ section for more details on possible team combinations.

    Can I choose players from one team only?
    A maximum of 5 players can be selected from each side

    Is the pricing for players reviewed from time to time on Star11fantasy?
    Yes, we review our player pricing from time to time and make changes to make the experience more exciting for you. Rest assured - to be absolutely fair to all our users, we’ll not make these changes while a match’s contests are open and getting filled.

    I’ve spent my entire budget before selecting all 7 players. How do I complete my team?
    That’s where your skill comes in! You need to create your team within the budget of 100 credits given to you. We’re sure you can do it!

    What is the use of a Captain & Vice-captain in the team?
    The Captains always leads from the front! Your team’s captain will receive 2 times the points, while Vice-captain will receive 1.5 times the points. So, choose your leaders carefully!

    How long does it take to reflect the changes I have made to my team?
    The changes are instant! You can make unlimited changes any time before the deadline hits for a particular match.

    Can I edit my team during the match?
    Come on, where’s the skill in that? You can create or edit your team only before the deadline, which is 1 hour prior to the start of the first match of the day. So, keep an eye on the clock!

    When do you update the squads for matches on Star11fantasy?
    The squads featured on Star11fantasy keep getting updated till the deadline for that particular match. We use reputed service providers that give us up-to-date information. We request you to keep checking for such updates till the deadline.

    How can I see my team’s rankings?
    You can click on the particular match for which you have created your team, click on the ''My Contests'' tab and then click on a particular contest to see the updated points and rankings for everyone participating.

    How do I create multiple teams for the same match?
    The ‘Multiple Team’ option is enabled after creating your first team. You can create a second team by clicking "Create Team 2" button which is displayed above the field preview.

    How many teams can I create in a matcb?
    You can create up to 6 separate teams for a single match

    Can I join the same contest using multiple teams?
    You can only join contests which display ‘Multi Entry’ with multiple teams. Otherwise, it’s one team per contest.

    Can I edit my team(s)?
    Yes! You can edit your team(s) as many times as you like before the match deadline.

    Can I choose which of my teams compete in a contest (Team 1 or Team 2) after joining?
    Yes! You can choose your team in a contest (Choose Team 1 or Team 2 to compete) from ‘My Contests’ tab until the deadline.
