  • Deposits
    • I deposited cash but it is not added to my Star11 account balance. Why?

      - There could have been an unexpected delay in updating your Star11 account balance. It may be the case that the bank has not cleared your transaction and we are yet to receive the amount.

      - In such scenarios, it can take up to 45 minutes for the transaction to be updated.

      - In case the amount does not get added even after 45 minutes, do not worry!! The transaction is possibly stuck and we do get notified by our Payment Gateway once it gets successful and the transaction is accordingly credited to your Star11 account. This usually happens when our Payment Gateway reconciles the transaction with the bank on the next working day.

      - In the meanwhile, please feel free to make another add cash transaction using an alternate mode like credit cards, debit cards, internet banking or wallets.

      Please be rest assured that your money is completely safe and will either get credited to your Star11 account or get refunded to your bank account/wallet. In case you still need our assistance, please write to us using the link below mentioning the date of the transaction and the transaction amount. Still need help? Write to us

    • Why am I unable to use my Rupay debit card?

      - Rupay debit cards also known as ATM cards are sometimes not enabled for online banking. Please check with your bank if your Rupay debit card is enabled for online transactions.

      - In the meanwhile, please use an alternate mode of payment like Credit card, Visa/Master/Maestro debit card, and PayTM wallet to deposit money into your Star11 account.

  • Withdrawals
    • How can I withdraw my winnings?
      You can follow the below steps to place a withdrawal query - Step 1: Login with your verified Star11 account - Step 2: Go to My Account and if your account is a verified account you will see a “Withdraw” option under the winnings section. - Step 3: Click Withdraw and enter the amount you would like to withdraw. You will receive a confirmation from Star11 on your registered email id. You can withdraw money only from the winnings balance once your Star11 account is verified. You cannot withdraw the cash amount but you can use it to join more contests and win more. This verification is a one-time process which doesn’t need to be repeated unless you wish to make changes to your account. We need you to provide the following bank account details: Name of the bank, Name of the branch, Name of the account holder, account no. and IFSC code. Once your account is verified, you can withdraw the desired amount (min. of Rs.200 and max. of Rs.2,00,000 at a time) and it will be deposited into your bank account within 5 working days.
    • Can I use someone else’s bank account to withdraw money?

      Unfortunately, you can withdraw money from your Star11 account balance ONLY to your own bank account. If you do not have a bank account, your winnings will keep getting accumulated in your Star11 account balance. Once you get a bank account in your name, you can use the same to get your Star11 account verified and place your withdrawal requests.

  • Points
    • When do the scores get updated?
      TPoints are usually updated every few minutes! However, when the match ends, the status changes from ‘In Progress’ to ‘Waiting For Review’. In this time, the final points including your team's total and your rank are updated. Winners are declared and winnings are distributed only after our team verifies the final points. This process may take up to 2 hours. In certain cases, it could take longer. After the winners are declared, the match status will be ‘Completed’.
    • How will I score points?
      You will score points depending on how the players in your team perform in the live match. So, make sure you pick a winning combination to help you win BIG! Check out our detailed Points System to understand how to score on Star11.
  • Playing the Game
    • How do I play the Star11 Sports Game?
      Step 1. Select a sport Step 2. Choose a match from the ‘Fixtures’ tab Step 3. Create your team within a virtual budget of 100 credits before the deadline Step 4. Now, join any of our Practice Contests to hone your skills or Cash Contests to win BIG Step 5. You can browse through the various cash contests at the match centre & join any of them. You can also create your own contest & invite friends to play with you. Once the match starts, your created team will score points based on the real-life performance of your chosen players in the match. Your rank in a cash contest is decided based on the points your team earns during the live match. If your team finishes amongst the winning ranks of that cash contest, you’ll be declared a winner in the contest. We’ll credit the winnings in your Star11 account, depending on the amount that you’ve won. Watch Video
    • How many teams can I create for a single match?
      You can create upto 6 different teams for a match and join a Multi-Entry contest with all 6 teams. However, every entry with a new team will require a separate entry fee. You can also choose to switch between the teams (team 1/team 2 etc.) before the deadline for every match.
  • Winnings
    • Why haven’t I received my winnings in my Star11 account balance yet?
      Usually, the winnings for a match are added to your account balance within 4 hours of the match being completed. However, for matches ending late at night, winners will be declared the next morning after which your winnings will be added to your Star11 account balance

  • Profile Changes
    • Can I change my TEAM NAME?
      Unfortunately, you CANNOT change your Team Name. Your Team Name is your unique identity on Star11. We believe in FairPlay and complete transparency, and allow everyone who’s joined a contest to track their opponents and see their points and ranking. If team names are frequently changed, it becomes very difficult to track performances and assess the competition.
    • Can I change my MOBILE NUMBER?
      Unfortunately, your mobile number CANNOT be changed once it is verified on your Star11 account.
